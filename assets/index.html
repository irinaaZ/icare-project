iCare+ Home Test Kit

Home Health Kits the starter program includes (6) FREE Home Test Kits, these should be merchandised as a cash offering to your customers. All lab results will automatically filter directly into the iCare+ mobile app for patients to view and will be available to pharmacist directly on the patient portal.
Re-reorders will be available for a cost as an Rx/OTC item inside the member catalog. Kits can be activated on site directly through the iCare+ patient-facing mobile application. Please instruct patients to open the kit, read insert and follow activation instruction.

Getting started:

  1. Your starter kits will automatically arrive inside a customized counter-top display packed with six unique test kits. The front-side of each kit is labeled by test type.
  2. Unwrap and display the kits inside your prescription drop off, pickup, or will call window.
  3. Either register GTIN by test type, and price inside your point-of-sale system or ring up as a cash sale by registering the suggested retail price.
  4. When compared to equivalent third-party lab testing, cost savings can be substantial.
  5. Patient lab results will filter directly into iCare+ mobile application, lab results and once shipped are typically available within 3-7 business days.
  6. Patient lab results filter directly into the provider portal, patient specific, lab results section.
  7. Questions? support@myicareplus.com
